Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2025

About This Policy

At AU Lotto, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information of users who engage with our website and services. Please read it carefully to understand how your data is managed.

Information We Collect

When you use AU Lotto, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details provided during account creation, such as name and email address
  • Technical data like IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Interaction data including site navigation, clicks, and engagement

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to enhance your AU Lotto experience. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Enable and maintain your account
  • Deliver personalized content and lottery features
  • Improve platform functionality and fix technical issues
  • Communicate important service updates

How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. Your information may be shared with trusted service providers that support AU Lotto operations (e.g., hosting, analytics, or email delivery services). All third-party partners are obligated to safeguard your data.

Cookies and Tracking

AU Lotto uses cookies to offer a secure and smooth browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences, track website usage, and improve functionality. For more details, view our Cookies Policy.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. When your data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also withdraw consent where applicable. To exercise your rights, please contact us directly using the details below.

Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While no system is entirely secure, we continuously monitor and enhance our security framework.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or service offerings. The revised date will be posted at the top of this page. We encourage you to review the policy regularly.
